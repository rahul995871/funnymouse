The Funny Mouse stands as the premier indoor play arena for children in Gurgaon. Our establishment boasts the finest equipment and meticulously designed interiors, all crafted by industry experts with the utmost consideration for children's comfort.
As the largest play arena in Gurgaon, spanning over 5,000 square feet, we take pride in offering an expansive and safe environment. Our professionally trained staff is dedicated to guiding and ensuring the well-being of every child. The funny Mouse features a state-of-the-art play arena, an engaging activity center, a vibrant party zone, and a dedicated toddler play area.
