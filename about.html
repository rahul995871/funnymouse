About Us

The Funny Mouse stands as the premier indoor play arena for children in Gurgaon. Our establishment boasts the finest equipment and meticulously designed interiors, all crafted by industry experts with the utmost consideration for children's comfort.
As the largest play arena in Gurgaon, spanning over 5000 square feet, we take pride in offering an expansive and safe environment. Our professionally trained staff is dedicated to guiding and ensuring the well-being of every child. The Funny Mouse features a state-of-the-art play arena, an engaging activity center, a vibrant party zone, and a dedicated toddler play area.
In addition to these exciting offerings, Our cafe provides a delightful culinary experience for both children and parents alike. Moreover, we take play to the next level with a selection of unique and captivating games and rides that set us apart from other play arenas in Delhi NCR. To enhance the overall experience, our entire play arena is air-conditioned and equipped with advanced air purification systems, creating a comfortable and enjoyable atmosphere for all."
